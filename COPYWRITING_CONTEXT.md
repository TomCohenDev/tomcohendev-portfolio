# Tom Cohen: Copywriting & Strategic Context

## 1. Business Identity: The "Product Studio" Model
You are not just a traditional dev agency. You are a **Product Studio** (similar to Thoughtbot, Railsware, or betaworks). 
- **Hybrid Approach:** You offer **Services** (Custom Dev, MVP Build) AND build your own **Products** (SaaS tools).
- **The "Why":** Because you build your own products, you understand the *business* of startups, not just the code. You "eat your own dog food."
- **Positioning:** "We build our own successful startups, so we know how to build yours."

## 2. Target Audience & Pain Points
### A. Early-Stage Founders (The Visionaries)
- **Pain:** Have an idea but no tech team. Afraid of wasting money on "spaghetti code" or agencies that don't "get it."
- **Need:** Speed, trust, and a partner who acts like a co-founder.
- **Solution:** "Rapid MVP Build" & "MVP Strategy."

### B. Scale-Ups (The Growers)
- **Pain:** Tech debt, slow feature delivery, need for specialized automation (n8n) or AI integration.
- **Need:** Reliability, high-quality engineering, complex problem solving.
- **Solution:** "Custom Development" & "n8n Automation."

## 3. Key Value Propositions (UVPs)
1.  **Founder-to-Founder Mindset:** You aren't a hired gun; you're a fellow founder. You optimize for *ROI* and *Growth*, not just billable hours.
2.  **End-to-end product journey:** From the first napkin sketch (Strategy) to the first 10k users (Scale/Automation).
3.  **Speed + Quality:** You use modern stacks (React, Supabase, n8n) to ship fast without breaking things.

## 4. Tone of Voice
- **Confident & Direct:** "Damn good at building products."
- **Pragmatic:** Focus on results (revenue, users, efficiency), not buzzwords.
- **Experienced:** You've been there. You know the pitfalls.
- **Partner-Oriented:** "Let's build together," not "Here is your quote."

## 5. Recommended Wording Strategy for Front Page

### Hero Section
- **Goal:** Instant credibility.
- **Headline:** Needs to be bold. (Current: "Damn good at building products" is strong).
- **Subhead:** Connect the "what" to the "value."
    - *Draft:* "A product studio for ambitious founders. We build custom software, automate workflows, and launch our own SaaS tools. We help you ship faster."

### Services Section (The "How")
- **For Startups:** Focus on *Validation* and *Speed*.
    - *Keywords:* Prototype in days, MVP, Investor-ready.
- **For Scale-ups:** Focus on *Stability* and *Automation*.
    - *Keywords:* Scalable architecture, AI integrations, 24/7 reliability.

### Products Section (The "Proof")
- **Goal:** Show, don't just tell. "We built this AI Agent, and it handles 80% of our support."
- **Message:** "Use the tools we use to win."

### About/Philosophy
- **Core Message:** "We don't just write code. We build businesses."
